Hey there, I am a PhD student advised by [Lawrence Kim](https://www.lhkim.com/) at [Simon Fraser University](https://www.sfu.ca/). I am a member of the [Tangent Lab](https://tangent.cs.sfu.ca/), which is part of the [ixlab](https://ixlab.cs.sfu.ca/). 

Previously, I got my BS degree in Computer Science at the [University of Wisconsin Madison](https://www.wisc.edu/), where I worked as an undergrad researcher with [Yaxin Hu](https://www.edayaxin.com/about.html) in [People and Robots Lab](https://peopleandrobots.wisc.edu/), advised by Prof. [Bilge Mutlu](https://bmutlu.github.io/)

My research focuses on Human-Robot Interaction and Human-AI Interaction. I am always open to new connections and potential collabrations, feel free to drop me an email!
